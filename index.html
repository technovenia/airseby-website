FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions answered

Why should I choose Air Profecto?

Air Profecto is a ground-breaking product that ensures to make your indoors free from harmful germs, bacteria, viruses, and other airborne contaminants, including COVID-19. It eliminates all types of harmful contaminants from the air so that you can enjoy clean and sanitized air without hassle.

Where to use Air Profecto?

You can use Air Profecto in all types of residential and commercial enclosed areas as well as transportation, including train stations, offices, homes, supermarkets, drama theatres, schools, planes, buses, subways, cruise ships etc. Moreover, you can use it in partially open spaces to secure places at satisfactory levels.

What are the Air Profecto's costs and the time it takes to install the product?

The system cost and installation time depend on your building size and application type in the building where you want to install the system.

Is its mobile application free of charge?

Yes, the mobile application of Air Profecto is free of charge and is available to work on iOS and android operating systems.
